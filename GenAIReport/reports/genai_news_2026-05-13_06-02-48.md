Engineers should watch this space closely as AI-driven interfaces promise to create more intuitive and context-aware user experiences, potentially redefining how we interact with digital tools.

### **[Show HN: Agentic interface for mainframes and COBOL](https://www.hypercubic.ai/hopper)**

For developers grappling with enterprise modernization, this agentic interface offers a powerful new paradigm for interacting with and extracting value from critical, long-standing infrastructure.
