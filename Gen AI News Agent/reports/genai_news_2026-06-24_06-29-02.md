### **[InSight: Self-Guided Skill Acquisition via Steerable VLAs - ArXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24884v1)**
The InSight framework introduces a novel approach for Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models to acquire skills through self-guidance, moving beyond the limitations of pre-existing training data. This research is critical for developing more autonomous and adaptable robotic systems capable of learning new manipulation skills on the fly, paving the way for more versatile and intelligent agents in unstructured environments.

### **[FLUX3D: High-Fidelity 3D Gaussian Generation with Diffusion-Aligned Sparse Representation - ArXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24874v1)**
FLUX3D presents a significant advancement in high-fidelity 3D Gaussian Splatting generation, addressing the challenge of preserving high-frequency visual details from input images. This work has profound implications for creating realistic 3D content, virtual reality, and digital twins, pushing the boundaries of generative AI in visual media and immersive experiences.

### **[OpenThoughts-Agent: Data Recipes for Agentic Models - ArXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24855v1)**
OpenThoughts-Agent explores the crucial, yet often opaque, aspect of curating training data for broadly capable agentic language models. This research is vital for understanding how to build more robust and versatile AI agents, providing "data recipes" that can unlock new levels of performance and application for autonomous AI systems. It's a foundational step towards more reliable agent development.

### **[Qwen-AgentWorld: Language World Models for General Agents - HackerNews](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24597)**
Qwen-AgentWorld introduces the concept of Language World Models for general agents, aiming to equip AI with a deeper understanding of the world to perform complex tasks. This research is a cornerstone for developing truly general-purpose AI agents that can reason, plan, and interact more effectively within diverse environments, moving closer to human-like intelligence.
