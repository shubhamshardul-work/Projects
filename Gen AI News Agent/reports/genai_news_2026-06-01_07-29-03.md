## 🏢 AI Business & Strategy

### **[SoftBank says it will invest up to €75 billion to build French data centers](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/30/softbank-says-it-will-invest-up-to-e75-billion-to-build-french-data-centers/)**
SoftBank's staggering €75 billion commitment to develop 5 gigawatts of data center capacity in France is a monumental bet on the future of AI infrastructure. This move underscores the escalating global demand for compute power and positions France as a strategic hub in the AI race, signaling a long-term vision for powering the next generation of AI innovation. Business leaders should note the intensifying competition for compute resources and the strategic importance of securing robust data center capabilities.

### **[Meta is reportedly developing an AI pendant](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/30/meta-is-reportedly-developing-an-ai-pendant/)**
Meta's rumored development of an AI pendant signifies a strategic pivot towards embedding AI directly into daily life through wearable technology, moving beyond its current focus on VR/AR. This initiative suggests a play for ubiquitous AI interaction and a new frontier for data collection and platform dominance. Executives should watch how this hardware push could redefine user interfaces and create new ecosystems for AI-powered services.

### **[Erin Brockovich takes aim at data center secrecy](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/31/erin-brockovich-takes-aim-at-data-center-secrecy/)**
Environmental activist Erin Brockovich's new mission targeting data center secrecy highlights a growing strategic risk for companies investing heavily in AI infrastructure. As data centers proliferate, concerns around energy consumption, water usage, and environmental impact are escalating. Business leaders must prioritize transparency, sustainability, and community engagement to mitigate regulatory challenges and reputational damage in this critical sector.

## 🛠️ AI Architects & Developers

### **[‘What a joke’: Github Copilot’s new token-based billing spurs consternation among devs](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/30/what-a-joke-github-copilots-new-token-based-billing-spurs-consternation-among-devs/)**
GitHub Copilot's shift to token-based billing is causing significant friction among developers, impacting cost predictability and potentially altering usage patterns for this popular AI coding assistant. Engineers need to re-evaluate their Copilot ROI, optimize their usage, and potentially explore alternative AI-powered development tools or strategies to manage costs effectively. This change signals a maturing market where AI tool providers are seeking more sustainable revenue models.

### **[ChatGPT for Google Sheets exfiltrates workbooks](https://www.promptarmor.com/resources/gpt-for-google-sheets-data-exfiltration)**
A critical security vulnerability has been identified where a "ChatGPT for Google Sheets" add-on can exfiltrate entire workbooks, posing a significant data security risk. Developers and practitioners must exercise extreme caution when integrating third-party AI tools, especially those requiring broad data access permissions. Prioritizing robust security audits, validating tool permissions, and educating users on potential vulnerabilities are paramount to prevent sensitive data breaches.

### **[1-Bit Bonsai Image 4B Image Generation for Local Devices](https://prismml.com/news/bonsai-image-4b)**
The introduction of 1-Bit Bonsai Image 4B offers a promising solution for efficient, local image generation on personal devices. This development is crucial for enabling privacy-preserving and low-latency AI applications without reliance on cloud infrastructure. Developers should watch this space for opportunities to integrate such lightweight, on-device models into edge computing, mobile applications, and other scenarios where data privacy and immediate processing are critical.

### **[The Speed of Prototyping in the Age of AI](https://darylcecile.net/notes/speed-of-prototyping-age-of-ai)**
This article highlights how AI is dramatically accelerating the prototyping phase of software development, allowing for rapid iteration and concept validation. Developers can leverage AI tools to quickly generate code, test ideas, and explore design possibilities, significantly reducing time-to-market. While embracing this speed, practitioners should also be mindful of maintaining code quality and avoiding "AI-driven technical debt" through careful review and architectural planning.

## 🔬 Research Frontiers

### **[Making sense of the debate over AI psychosis](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/31/making-sense-of-the-debate-over-ai-psychosis/)**
The ongoing debate over "AI psychosis" delves into the profound psychological and cognitive implications of deep human-AI interaction, particularly among tech leaders. Researchers are exploring whether intense engagement with AI can lead to cognitive biases, altered perceptions, or even delusional thinking. This frontier demands interdisciplinary research into the long-term effects of AI on human cognition, decision-making, and mental well-being, moving beyond technical capabilities to understand the human element.
